Time With My Son

By Kaysan Thomas

I created this animation inspired by one of the most meaningful relationships in my life, my son, who came into my world two years ago. Every day we spend time together, and one of our favorite things to do is play. Lately, he's developed a love for basketball, whether we're shooting hoops at home or outside. I wanted to capture what it feels like to hang out with him, those simple, joyful moments that mean everything.
